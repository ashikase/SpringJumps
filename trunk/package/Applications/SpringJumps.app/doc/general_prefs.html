Page titles
(Default: On)

  • Off:
    Do not show page titles.
  • On:
    For pages that have the associated jump icon enabled, show a title at the top of the page. The title will match the name of the jump icon.

Jump dock
(Default: Off)

  • Off:
    Jump icons appear as regular icons within SpringBoard.
  • On:
    The jump icons are stored in a popup view, accessible by tapping the page indicator dots just above the SpringBoard dock bar.
